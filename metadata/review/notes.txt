ParentReply requires iOS 26 or later with Apple Intelligence enabled.

The app uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework (FoundationModels) to generate reply suggestions and Vision framework for OCR text extraction from screenshots. Both run entirely on-device.

TEST FLOW:
1. Open the app — you will see the onboarding flow (7 screens)
2. Complete onboarding or tap "Start free" on the paywall
3. On the Home screen, tap "Choose Screenshot"
4. Select any screenshot containing a school-related message (email, text, ClassDojo, etc.)
5. The app extracts text via Vision OCR, then generates 6 reply options in different tones
6. The "Grateful" tone is free; other tones and the situation summary require a Pro subscription
7. Tap any unlocked reply card to copy the text (2-minute clipboard expiry)

FREE TIER:
- Grateful tone always available
- 5 analyses per calendar week
- Situation summary is blurred (teaser)

PRO TIER:
- All 6 tones unlocked
- Unlimited analyses
- Full situation summary visible

No network requests are made. No user data leaves the device. No third-party SDKs are included.
