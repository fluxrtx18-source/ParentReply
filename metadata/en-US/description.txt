ParentReply helps parents reply to school messages with confidence — in seconds, not minutes of overthinking.

Screenshot a message from your child's teacher, ClassDojo, Remind, or school email. ParentReply reads it and generates six parent-appropriate replies in different tones — so you can pick the one that fits and send it immediately.

WHAT MAKES PARENTREPLY DIFFERENT

- 6 Tones, 1 Tap: Get six ready-to-send replies in Grateful, Concerned, Supportive, Diplomatic, Firm, and Clarifying tones. Copy your favorite and paste it into any app.

- AI Picks the Best Tone: Not sure how to respond? ParentReply highlights the tone that fits the situation — so you never have to guess.

- Situation Summary: Instantly understand what the school needs from you before you reply. See at a glance whether the teacher expects a thank-you, a meeting, or a decision.

- 100% On-Device AI: Powered by Apple's Foundation Models. Your messages never leave your iPhone. No cloud. No data collection. Complete privacy.

- Screenshot to Reply: No typing or pasting needed. Just share a screenshot and get six replies in seconds.

WHO IT'S FOR

Parents, guardians, and caregivers who communicate with teachers, schools, and educational platforms — especially those who overthink every word, aren't sure what tone is appropriate, or want to reply quickly without sounding like a form letter.

PRICING

- Free: Grateful tone + 5 replies per week
- ParentReply Pro: All 6 tones, situation summary, unlimited replies — $2.99/week, $6.99/month, or $29.99/year

PRIVACY FIRST

ParentReply uses Apple's on-device AI. Your school messages are never uploaded, stored on servers, or shared with third parties. What happens on your iPhone stays on your iPhone.
