Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

ParentReply ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

ParentReply processes all school message text and AI-generated replies entirely on your device using Apple's Foundation Models and Vision frameworks. We do not:

  • Upload your school messages or screenshots to any server
  • Store your message text on any cloud infrastructure
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or analytics profiling
  • Access your contacts, location, camera, or microphone

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your iPhone:

  • Usage counter: A count and date stamp tracking your weekly free reply quota. No personal data is included.
  • Onboarding state: Whether you have completed onboarding (a boolean flag).
  • Subscription cache: A boolean indicating your subscription status, used to display the correct UI.

All local data can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

3. Photo Library Access

ParentReply requests access to your photo library solely so you can select screenshots of school messages for analysis. Selected images are processed on-device by Apple's Vision framework for text extraction. Images are never stored by the app, cached, or transmitted anywhere.

4. On-Device AI Processing

ParentReply uses two Apple frameworks that run entirely on your device:

  • Vision (VNRecognizeTextRequest): Extracts text from your screenshot using on-device OCR.
  • Foundation Models (LanguageModelSession): Generates reply suggestions using Apple's on-device language model.

Neither framework sends data to any external server. Processing happens on your iPhone's Neural Engine.

5. Subscriptions

ParentReply Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. Subscription management is handled by Apple's StoreKit framework.

6. Clipboard

When you tap a reply card, the text is copied to your clipboard with a 2-minute expiration. After 2 minutes, the clipboard entry is automatically removed by iOS. We do not read from or monitor your clipboard at any other time.

7. Third-Party Services

ParentReply does not integrate any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs. The app uses only Apple's first-party frameworks.

8. Children's Privacy

ParentReply is designed for parents and guardians, not children. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and in the app's App Store listing.
