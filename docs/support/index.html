Frequently Asked Questions

How does ParentReply work?

Take a screenshot of any school message (email, ClassDojo, Remind, text). Open ParentReply and select the screenshot from your photo library. The app reads the text using on-device OCR, then generates six reply options in different tones — all in seconds.

How does the on-device AI work?

ParentReply uses Apple's Foundation Models framework, which runs entirely on your iPhone's Neural Engine. Your school messages are processed locally — they never leave your device and are never sent to any external server.

What tones are available?

ParentReply generates replies in six tones: Grateful (warm, thankful), Concerned (expressing worry), Supportive (volunteering to help), Diplomatic (navigating disagreements), Firm (setting boundaries), and Clarifying (requesting more info). The AI also suggests which tone best fits each message.

What can I use for free?

Free users get the Grateful tone and up to 5 analyses per week. The situation summary and the other 5 tones are available with a ParentReply Pro subscription. No credit card is needed for the free tier.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

All subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel, open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions, then ParentReply Pro, then Cancel Subscription. You can also manage it from the App Store.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open ParentReply and tap "Upgrade" in the top-right corner of the home screen. On the paywall, tap "Restore Purchases." As long as you're signed in with the same Apple ID, your Pro subscription will be restored automatically.

Why did the copied text disappear from my clipboard?

For privacy, ParentReply sets a 2-minute expiration on copied reply text. After 2 minutes, iOS automatically clears it from your clipboard. This prevents school messages from lingering silently after you switch apps.

The app isn't generating replies. What should I check?

ParentReply requires iOS 26 or later on a supported device with Apple Intelligence enabled. If replies aren't generating, make sure your device is up to date and that on-device AI features are enabled in Settings under Apple Intelligence.

How do I delete my data?

All ParentReply data is stored locally on your device. To delete everything, simply uninstall the app. No data is stored on any server, so there's nothing to request deletion of remotely.
